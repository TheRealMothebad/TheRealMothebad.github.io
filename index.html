The Abridged Biography Of Guillaume Amontons!

Guillaume Amontons was a french physicist who is probably best known for his research into friction, which produced Amontons three laws of friction.

Five Facts:

- Was born on August 31st, 1663, in Paris France. He became deaf in third grade.

- Developed Amontons three laws of friction.

-Designed the first sterling engine, "Amonton's fire mill"

- Was recomended for the position “Membre Premier titulaire” of the reformed acadamy by a friend, and was aproved.

- Sadly, Amontons died a few days later, on october 11th, 1705 from peritonitis. and never got to hear the news of his appointment.

Amontons Three laws of friction:

1. The force of friction is directly proportional to the applied load.
2. The force of friction is independent of the apparent area of contact.
3. Kinetic friction is independent of the sliding velocity.
